Welcome to the office of Dr. Cho. Proudly serving Richmond Hill, ON and other surrounding areas. At CHO ORTHODONTICS, Dr. Janice Cho is extremely proud to provide you with the smile you deserve! It is our goal to provide excellent orthodontic care in a friendly, caring, professional environment. We provide comprehensive, patient-centred orthodontic care for children, teens and adults. Dr. Cho strives to achieve excellent patient care and an outstanding patient experience through personal interactions with our patients, gentle treatment, well-trained staff, and beautiful results. Stop in and visit our modern office, where we hope you will feel relaxed.


Our passion is to make a profound impact on the lives of our patients by utilizing the latest orthodontic techniques and practices. There is nothing more rewarding than seeing improved self-confidence in our patients and the fun they have in our office.


Orthodontist have 2-3 years of specialized training following dental school to ensure proper form and function while straightening teeth. Our state-of-the-art office allows us to provide a unique orthodontic experience accompanied by our extensive orthodontist experience with Invisalign, Invisalign teen, clear invisible braces and braces for children and adults alike. We are pleased to offer the following specialized services:


  • Braces for children, teens, and adults
  • Invisalign / clear aligners
  • Clear braces
  • Lingual braces

Our professional and friendly staff helps to create a warm environment where quality of patient care is our top priority. Call our office today for your complimentary consultation and find out what a difference a healthy smile can make in your life.



We recognize that every patient has different needs, and we pride ourselves in the courteous service we deliver to each person who walks through our doors. Whether it be children or adult, our knowledgeable team is committed to helping you achieve a healthy, beautiful smile!
